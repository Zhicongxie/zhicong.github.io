Research Experience

Research project (Thesis) in bachelor

During my undergraduate education, I worked in the department's heavy metal geochemisty researh group. This fostered my interest in biogeochemical cyces. Therefore, my thesis is about phytoremediation: Two kinds of Seldum alfredii (Hyperaccumulating and non- Hyperaccumulating ecotypes) were selected as the research material, used high performance liquid chromatography (HPLC) to analyze the organic acid components of rhizosphere soil and non-rhizosphere soil. In addition, the variation of microbial community structure across differennt concentrations was also been explored.

Internship in research institution


My internship in the Institute of Atmospheric Environment of Guangdong Provincial Academy of Environmental Science as a research assistant has further prompted my aspiration for combining modern technologies with environmental issue research and let me realize in what aspects I need to make progress. During the half-year period, I have learned a lot about atmospheric and carbon cycle topics involving all sorts of aspects. For example, I participated in two projects on environmental policies. One was an urban ‘Three Lines and One List’ project which meant to settle the ecological protection baseline, environmental quality bottom line, resource utilization upper limit and ecological environment access list; the other one was to make an emission inventory (data process). For practice, I took part in the calculation of aircraft exhaust emissions in the Green Airport Project and the comparison of vehicle exhaust data in the motor vehicle remote sensing monitoring data project.

Lab work in detection company

I have worked in a detection technology company as a laboratory inspector. This enhanced my practical ability to conduct chemical experiments, gave me the potential capabilities to perform laboratory works.
